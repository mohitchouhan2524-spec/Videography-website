About the studio

Two storytellers, one lens, five years of frames worth keeping.

2Bclicks began with a simple belief: a wedding, a brand, a single reel — every story deserves to be shot like a film, not just recorded.

For more than 5 years, Ankit and Mohit have built 2Bclicks around that idea — Ankit behind the camera, reading light and moments as they happen; Mohit behind the timeline, shaping raw footage into something that feels inevitable. Based in Indore and working across Dewas, Dhar and Ujjain, they've shot everything from intimate haldi mornings to full commercial campaigns.
